# Saturdaze — Family Weekend Planner

A friendly web app that plans Saturday + Sunday for a Port Credit family of four, around fixed commitments (swimming, church, bedtime, husband's workout) and known preferences (parks, Rec Room, Zoo, theatre, hikes, lavender fields, short drives, picky restaurant choices).

## Core idea
- One-tap "Plan my weekend" → a ready-to-go Saturday + Sunday itinerary
- Respects fixed events, kid ages (9 & 5), 9pm bedtime, 9am out-the-door, no camping
- Suggests outings, food, and downtime — not a generic calendar

## Screens

### 1. Home / This Weekend
- Big "Plan This Weekend" button
- Weather strip for Sat + Sun
- Two day cards (Sat / Sun) with the proposed itinerary
- Swap, lock, or regenerate any block

### 2. Family Profile (one-time setup, editable)
- Family members + ages
- Home location (Port Credit, Mississauga)
- Recurring commitments (Sat swim, Sun church, husband's workout window)
- Likes / dislikes (e.g. no camping)
- Budget: not a constraint (toggle on/off)

### 3. Itinerary Detail (per day)
- Timeline view: morning → evening, kids in bed by 9pm
- Blocks: workout, activity, meal, drive time, downtime
- Tap a block → why it was suggested, alternatives, map link

### 4. Activity Suggestions
- Curated for our family: parks, Rec Room, Zoo, circus (when in town), live theatre, hikes, lavender fields, short drives
- Filter by: indoor/outdoor, drive time, kid-friendly age range, weather-appropriate
- "Try something new" toggle for variety

### 5. Restaurant Picker
- Tackles the food debate
- Pre-filtered to wife-approved style (set during profile)
- Lunch + dinner slots, near today's activity
- Quick vote / veto from each family member

### 6. Saved Weekends / History
- See past weekends, mark favourites, repeat or remix
- Avoid repeating last weekend's activity automatically

### 7. Local Events Feed
- What's on this weekend within X minutes' drive
- Circus, theatre, festivals, seasonal (lavender bloom, pumpkin patch, etc.)

### 8. Shopping Errand Slot (optional)
- Wife flags a needed shopping run; planner fits it into the weekend without killing the day

## Nice-to-haves (later)

- Share itinerary with wife for approval
- Kid view: pictures of what we're doing today
