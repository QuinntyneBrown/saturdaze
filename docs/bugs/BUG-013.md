# BUG-013 — Splash hero "Create your account" CTA has no action

**Status:** Fixed
**Severity:** Medium
**Opened:** 2026-05-17
**Source:** [button-link-audit-2026-05-17.md §Splash](../button-link-audit-2026-05-17.md)
**Area:** splash / onboarding

## Summary

The hero **Create your account** button on `/` produces no visible state
change. It's a duplicate of the splash's main signup CTA and equally inert.

## Steps to reproduce

1. Open <https://gentle-rock-070070c0f.7.azurestaticapps.net/>.
2. Scroll to the hero section.
3. Click **Create your account**.
4. Observe: URL unchanged, no dialog, no network activity.

## Expected

Click navigates to `/signup`.

## Actual

Nothing happens.

## Likely source

- `frontend/projects/saturdaze/src/app/pages/splash/splash.page.html`
- `frontend/projects/saturdaze/src/app/pages/splash/splash.page.ts`

## Acceptance

- Click navigates to `/signup`.
- Playwright spec asserts both the hero and closing **Create your account**
  buttons land on `/signup`.

## Notes

Pair with [BUG-012](BUG-012.md) and [BUG-015](BUG-015.md) — they're all
signup CTAs on the same page. Consider extracting a shared `signUp()`
handler.
