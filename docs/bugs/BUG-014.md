# BUG-014 — Splash hero "See a sample weekend" CTA has no defined behaviour

**Status:** Fixed
**Severity:** Medium
**Opened:** 2026-05-17
**Source:** [button-link-audit-2026-05-17.md §Splash](../button-link-audit-2026-05-17.md)
**Area:** splash / onboarding

## Summary

The hero secondary CTA **See a sample weekend** on `/` is rendered but
does nothing on click. Unlike the signup CTAs, the *intended* behaviour of
this button is not defined — it could be a public sample weekend route, a
modal preview, or a login bounce — and the product owner has to make that
call before the button is wired.

## Steps to reproduce

1. Open <https://gentle-rock-070070c0f.7.azurestaticapps.net/>.
2. Click **See a sample weekend**.
3. Observe: no route, no dialog, no API call, no state change.

## Expected (decision required)

One of the following, depending on product intent:

- Route to a public `/sample-weekend` page rendered from canned data.
- Open a modal preview of a sample weekend.
- Route to `/login?returnUrl=%2Fweekend` if the sample shouldn't be public.

## Actual

Nothing happens.

## Likely source

- `frontend/projects/saturdaze/src/app/pages/splash/splash.page.html`
- `frontend/projects/saturdaze/src/app/pages/splash/splash.page.ts`

## Acceptance

- Product owner picks one of the three behaviours.
- Button is wired to that behaviour with a Playwright spec.
- Until a decision is made, hide the button rather than ship an inert CTA.
