How a Saturdaze weekend comes together

Three small moments, and the logistics disappear.

Step 01

Tell us who's in the family

Names, ages, what each kid is into, the things you've all agreed to avoid. A minute of setup, paid back every weekend.

Step 02

Lock the things that don't move

Saturday swim, Sunday church, the weekly Costco run — Saturdaze treats them as anchors and plans around them, not over them.

Step 03

Get a weekend, every Friday

Friday at 6pm, a draft of both days lands — weather-checked, drive-time-checked, kid-tested. Tap to swap anything you don't love.

Step 04

Send it to your partner

A read-only preview link your partner can open without an account. Approve, suggest a swap, or just say "yep, perfect."
