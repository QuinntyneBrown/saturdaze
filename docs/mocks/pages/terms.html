4. What I do

Saturdaze drafts weekend plans for your family based on what you've told me about the kids, your home base, your locked commitments, and what's worked before. The drafts are suggestions — you decide what actually happens.

Some recommendations point you toward businesses (restaurants, parks, ticketed venues). I don't get paid for those; the algorithm picks based on fit, not partnerships.

5. Closing your account

You can close your account from your profile any time. Closure deletes your data within 30 days; backups roll off within 90.

I can close an account for misuse — fraud, scraping, harassment, that sort of thing. If it's a mistake, reply to the closure email and we'll talk.

The service is provided "as is". I'll do my best to keep it running but can't promise zero downtime.
