1. What I collect

Two kinds of things — what you give me, and what your browser sends just by being there.

2. Why I collect it

To plan weekends, send the Friday 6pm preview, and improve recommendations for you. That's it. No advertising, no third-party data sale, no model training.

3. A note on kids' data

Kids show up here only as first names and ages — the bare minimum needed to plan age-appropriate activities. Nothing more is collected from or about them.

Children don't have their own logins. The parent account is the only entry point. If your child is on your family list and they grow out of needing to be planned for, remove them from your family profile any time.

4. When I share it

Three narrow cases:

  • When you share. The "Share this weekend" link is a read-only preview you create; recipients only see what's on it.
  • Service providers. Hosting (Azure, Canada Central region), email delivery (Postmark), and analytics (Plausible — no cookies, no personal IDs).
  • Legal. If compelled by a Canadian court order. I publish a transparency report annually.

5. Where it lives

All family data is stored in Azure's canadacentral region. The database is encrypted at rest; connections are TLS only. Operational backups roll off after 90 days.

6. Your rights

From your profile you can:

  • Export — download everything as JSON.
  • Delete — close the account; all data is removed within 30 days, backups within 90.
  • Correct — edit any family detail any time.

Under PIPEDA (Canada) and GDPR (EU), you also have the right to access, rectification, erasure, and to object to processing. Email us — we don't make you fill out forms for this.

7. Cookies and analytics

We use one functional cookie — your sign-in token. No tracking cookies, no third-party cookies, no advertising IDs.

For analytics we use Plausible, which is cookieless and counts only aggregate page views. No individual user can be identified from it.

8. Contact

Privacy questions go to privacy@saturdaze.app. Real human reply within a day or two.
