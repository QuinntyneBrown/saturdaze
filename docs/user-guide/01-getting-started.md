# 1. Getting Started

This chapter answers the basics: what Saturdaze is, what it gives you,
and what to expect the first time you open it.

## What Saturdaze does, in one sentence

Saturdaze turns "we have no plan this weekend" into a ready-to-use
Saturday and Sunday for your family, in about one tap.

## What you get

Every weekend, Saturdaze hands you:

- A **timeline for Saturday** and a **timeline for Sunday**, from morning
  through evening
- The fixed stuff already in place (swim, church, your workout, bedtime)
- **Activity ideas** picked for your kids' ages, the weather, and how
  much driving you feel like
- **Restaurant suggestions** that fit your family's taste — including
  the picky eaters
- **Drive times** so you know what you're really signing up for
- **Downtime blocks**, on purpose, so the day doesn't feel like a forced
  march
- A **heads-up panel** that points out useful things (e.g. "the lavender
  is peaking" or "rain moves in Monday")

You can take the whole plan as-is, swap any block, lock the ones you
love, or shake the whole weekend and start over.

## What it does *not* do

A few things to set expectations:

- It is **not a generic calendar**. It will not show you your work
  meetings or your kid's piano recital unless that recital is set up
  as a family commitment in your profile.
- It is **not a booking engine**. It will recommend restaurants and
  activities, but reservations and tickets are still on you.
- It is **not strict**. If you ignore its plan and go to the beach,
  the app will not be hurt.

## The first time you open the app

The first time you open Saturdaze, you will be walked through three
quick steps:

1. **Add your family.** Names and ages for everyone — adults and kids.
   See [Setting Up Your Family](02-setting-up-your-family.md).
2. **Set your home location.** Most families just leave this on Port
   Credit. Drive-time suggestions are measured from here.
3. **Add your fixed weekend commitments.** Swim lessons, church, your
   workout window, anything else that always happens.

Once those are in, the home screen unlocks the big **Plan This Weekend**
button. Tap it, and you're off.

## The four areas of the app

Look at the bottom of the screen. You will always see four buttons:

| Button         | What lives here                                          |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Home**       | This weekend's plan, weather, and one-tap actions        |
| **Activities** | A browsable library of things to do near you             |
| **Saved**      | Past weekends you marked as favourites                   |
| **Profile**    | Your family setup, preferences, and recurring stuff      |

Everything in this guide happens inside one of those four areas.
